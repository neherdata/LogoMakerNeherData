PLP

ONLY THE PLP WILL DELIVER A SECURE FUTURE FOR ALL THE PEOPLE OF ST KITTS AND NEVIS. A SECURE FUTURE WITH A GROWING ECONOMY, REAL PROTECTION FOR THE PEOPLE AND MORE JOBS AND OPPORTUNITIES FOR OUR YOUNG PEOPLE.

A GROWING ECONOMY

Timothy Harris and his PLP team have a strong record of growing the economy and protecting the people of St Kitts and Nevis. Our strong economic management is creating more jobs, providing real opportunities for our young people, protecting us against external threats, and delivering successful government programmes.

Imagine where we would have been if Timothy Harris did not pay off Labour’s sky-high IMF debt. With daily predictions of a global recession, there is too much at stake to risk your livelihood on inexperienced or lazy leadership.

PROTECTING OUR PEOPLE

The PLP has a strong record of protecting the people. Be it by making our streets safer after Labour’s neglect, growing the economy so people were financially protected during the COVID crisis, or with life changing programmes like PAP and STEP.

Prime Minister Timothy Harris and his PLP team have a strong economic plan to keep the economy growing so our people can be protected and to build on the peace, enhancing and improving it to ensure it never falls apart.

CREATING MORE JOBS

Only with strong economic management can we create the jobs and opportunities our young people need . Other politicians simply don’t show up to work or have no economic experience. This would lead to less jobs and less opportunities.

The PLP will create more jobs, by continuing to support new industries and entrepreneurs, keeping costs low and championing opportunities for young people.

Representing You

Our local candidates are a team of genuine community members and small business owners, who know the importance of delivering locally and being answerable to their local community.
